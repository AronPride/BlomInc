Blóm is the Old Norse word for flower.

Our name is inspired by Nordic heritage, craftsmanship, and our reverence for the flowers and plants that have enriched human life for generations.

Founded in Minnesota, Blom combines scientific rigor, disciplined cultivation, and thoughtful manufacturing to produce cannabis of exceptional quality and consistency. Every detail, from genetics and environment to harvest and processing, is approached with the precision of an engineer and the care of a grower.
